About Us

We Use State of the Art Machinery to Grade and Sort our Medjool Dates.

Palmera has started production of its finest Medjool dates from Jordanian and Palestinian crops in its new facility in Amman Jordan, using a state of the art technology provided by Elisam Srl. The new grading machine provide excellent accuracy in grading our Medjool dates inclusive of size, quality, moisture and internal mold.
